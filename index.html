Recast is the United Kingdom's first live intelligence platform for demolition activity and reclaimed construction materials — mapping what is coming down so that what is worth saving can be found before it is lost.

Every week, buildings are stripped and their materials are landfilled. Not because nobody wanted them — but because nobody knew in time. Recast closes that gap.
