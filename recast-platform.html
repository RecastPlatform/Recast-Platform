About Recast

A live atlas of demolition. A marketplace for what remains.

Recast is a comprehensive index and interactive atlas of live and planned demolition projects across the UK. We map what is coming down — so that what is worth saving can be found, listed, and sourced before it is lost.

The platform connects architects, contractors, developers, and specialists with verified reclaimed materials directly from active sites. Every listing is tied to a real project, a real timeline, and a real point of contact.

Our ambition is to make the circular use of construction materials the default — not the exception.
